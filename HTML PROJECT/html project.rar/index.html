ELITE HOSPITAL is open to all patients every day and provides fundamental medical care and cutting-edge medicine in a central location in major cities in India. We use our superior academic knowledge to treat a wide range of health issues, taking a personal touch and utilizing highly specialized and up-to-date research. Each year, we treat around 10,000 inpatients and have over 30,000 outpatient visits at our 5 departments and institutes. Our employees guarantee comprehensive and individualized medical support even beyond their specific disciplines. With 750 hospital beds and close to 30000 inpatient days, ELITE is one of the largest hospitals in India.

We research the latest treatment methods and apply them according to strict scientific criteria. We are the sole provider of many therapies in India. Patients with complex and multiple health problems travel from all over the country and abroad to be treated here. With regard to research and education, the ELITE is closely affiliated with the Lovely Professional of medical school and laboratories. We have numerous collaborative projects with AIMS. This puts the ELITE at the forefront of medical research in India and enjoys an excellent international reputation. Our professors travel worldwide to congresses in order to exchange experience and expertise.
